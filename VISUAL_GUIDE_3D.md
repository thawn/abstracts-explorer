## Benefits of 3D

1. **More Information**: Uses 3 dimensions instead of 2
   - Captures more variance from the embedding space
   - Better representation of the data

2. **Better Separation**: Clusters that overlap in 2D may be separated in 3D
   ```
   2D View:           3D View:
   â—â—â—â—â—â—             â—â—â—â—  (above)
   â—â—â—â—â—â—             â—â—â—â—  (below)
   (Overlapping)      (Separated)
   ```

3. **Interactive Exploration**: Rotation reveals hidden patterns
   - View from different angles
   - Understand spatial relationships
   - Discover cluster proximity

4. **Professional**: Modern visualization matching research standards
   - Many ML papers use 3D embeddings
   - Familiar to researchers
   - More informative for presentations
